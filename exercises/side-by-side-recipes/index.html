BLANKET DUMPLINGS

Prep Time: 12 minutes Total Time: 25 minutes Cook Time: 12 minutes Yeilds: 10 Dumplings

This is my take on the viral blanket dumplings where you skip all the folding and just press the wrapper right over the filling. The bottoms get crispy, the tops stay soft, and the filling is juicy, spicy, and packed with flavor from the kimchi and gochujang. It’s kind of like an upside-down shumai with a crispy base and a soft, steamy top.
