the 1975 thriller Jaws, a massive great white shark terrorizes the New England tourist destination of Amity Island. Police Chief Martin Brody, marine biologist Matt Hooper, and grizzled fisherman Quint team up to hunt the beast after the mayor refuses to close the beaches for the summer.

The Mummy

Genre action and adventure Run time 2hr 10min Rating 7.1/10 stars

Synopsis

In 1925 Egypt, adventurer Rick O'Connell, clumsy Egyptologist Evelyn Carnahan, and her brother Jonathan journey to Hamunaptra, the City of the Dead. They accidentally awaken Imhotep, a cursed mummified high priest. Imhotep unleashes the Plagues of Egypt in a quest to resurrect his lost love, forcing the heroes to stop him

Star Wars the Force awakens

Synopsis Genre Action/adventure Run time 2hr 18min Rating 7.7/10 stars

Set thirty years after Return of the Jedi, The Force Awakens follows the Resistance and the villainous First Order as they race to find the missing Jedi Master, Luke Skywalker. The conflict introduces new heroes—a scavenger named Rey and a defecting stormtrooper named Finn—who team up with Han Solo and Chewbacca.

Tangled

Genre Family/Adventure/Romans Run time 1hr 40min Rating 7.7/10 stars

Synopsis

Disney's Tangled follows Princess Rapunzel, who was abducted as a baby by the manipulative Mother Gothel. Gothel locks her in a hidden tower to exploit the healing magic of Rapunzel's 70-foot golden hair. On her 18th birthday, Rapunzel escapes with an outlaw named Flynn Rider to see the kingdom's floating lanterns.
